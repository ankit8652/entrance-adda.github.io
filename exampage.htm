MHT CET Mock Test Marking Scheme

Candidates taking the MHT CET 2021 must be aware of the marking scheme. According to the MHT CET exam pattern 2021, students will be given one mark for each correct answer in Paper I (Physics and Chemistry), and two marks for each correct answer in Paper-II (Mathematics). There will be no negative marking, and no marks will be deducted for unattempted answers.
